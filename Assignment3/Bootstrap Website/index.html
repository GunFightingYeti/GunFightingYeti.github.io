About Me

Tim Lewis

From July 2018 to January 2019, I went through Denver University's Coding Bootcamp. This is a rigorous curriculum that covers a full stack, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, PHP, Laravel, Express.js, React.js, Database Theory, Bookshelf.js, MongoDB, MySQL, Command Line, Git, and more.

Over the last two years I have been working in the field of maintenance solving different types of problems and puzzles. Working for Spectrum Retirement as a Director of Maintenance, the focus is running a senior living community and making sure that everything runs smoothly and the residents are happy.

Prior to that, as a Krav Maga instructor for 5 years, the job included everything from teaching one on one classes to international trainings instructing groups of 100+ people. I hold two 2nd degree black belts. The first is in Premier Martial Arts which is a Mixed Martial Arts system comprised of Krav Maga, Muay Thai, American Kickboxing, and Brazillian Jui Jitsu. The second is in Kukkiwon, a Korean governed body of Tae Kwon Do. Since 2001, training in multiple systems, I have earned rank in six different martial arts.

I am extremely hard working and care greatly about impacting the surrounding community for the better with my daily actions. My core values are honesty and integrity above all else and are the guiding principles in my work and life. I strive to produce high quality results, no matter what I am working towards. Skill sets in varying backgrounds, including; IT, Retail, Construction, Customer Service, Landscaping, Teaching and Management contributes to my ability to adapt quickly. No challenge is too small and no puzzle is unsolvable.

"You cannot fail if you never give up. - Abraham Lincoln"
